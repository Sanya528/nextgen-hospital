Our Mission

Our mission is to build an intelligent and secure digital healthcare platform that enhances symptom understanding, supports early disease prediction, and simplifies patient data management using Artificial Intelligence and cloud technologies.

Next-Gen Hospital enables users to manage medical profiles, predict possible health conditions from symptoms, and book doctor appointments through a smart, user-friendly system designed to improve healthcare access and awareness.

  • Secure Patient Medical Profiles
  • AI-Based Symptom & Disease Prediction
  • Online Doctor Appointment Booking
  • Admin-Controlled Doctor Management
  • AWS-Powered Cloud Infrastructure

Key Usage Scenarios

Symptom-Based Disease Guidance

Helps users understand possible diseases based on symptom descriptions using AI.

Patient Health Awareness

Encourages early awareness and better understanding of personal health conditions.

Doctor Appointment Management

Allows easy scheduling and management of doctor appointments through the platform.

Medical AI Research & Learning

Supports academic research, experimentation, and learning in healthcare AI systems.

Clinical Decision Support Demo

Demonstrates AI-assisted decision-making as a prototype for clinical environments.

Built on AWS

  • AWS IAM — Role creation & permission management for EC2, DynamoDB, and SNS
  • Amazon DynamoDB — Cloud database for patient records and appointments
  • AWS SNS — Notification service for alerts and messages
  • Amazon EC2 — Hosting and deployment of the Flask application
